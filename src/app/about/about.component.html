Wizards vs Pirates

This is a card battling game. You pick either Pirates or Wizards. Then, you play the cards from your hand to one of five lanes. If there's an enemy across, you do your card's damage to the enemy card's health. If there's no card across, you will do damage to the enemy mothership. The winner is the first one to either kill all of the other person's cards or to destroy the enemy mothership.
